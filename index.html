About Our Charging Network

Growing the EV Chargepoint network is a key step towards a more sustainable and environmentally friendly future. A larger network means more EV Charging Stations, making it easier for drivers to find a charging point when needed, reducing 'range anxiety'- the fear that an electric vehicle has insufficient range to reach its destination. Supporting strategically located charging stations provide easy access for EV owners, making both long-distance and charging on-to-go a hassle-free experience anywhere.
